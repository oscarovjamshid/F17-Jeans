About Us

Vision & Mission

 The Company Vision is to grow into an internationally extrusive denim textile company and be graded among the paramount’s in the globe in terms of excellence, serviceability and above all satisfaction of customers.


The Company Mission is to revolutionize Uzbekistan’s denim apparel value chain with refined supply chain management and investments in state-of-the-art infrastructure and human capital development to deliver with innovation, speed, and agility.


At F17 Jeans, we perform in unison by following our values and ethical guidelines code of conduct as a source of guidance and inspiration, which enable us to achieve our both vision and mission.


Sample & Product Development

 All garment begins with PATTERN! A good pattern making is the crucial link to determine the garment, especially the denims of which wash requires the precise shrinkage rate of fabric. Hence the pattern making should build in the rate before wash to allow shrinkage after then to achieve accurate spec measurement for each pair of jeans.


It will then leave to our skilled MASTERS’s hands for sewing. 7 skilled workers are creating pieces of arts every day here in our sampling room.


Each piece is strictly qualified before handover to our customers hand. We give all our best efforts for correct and perfect sample making to get satisfaction from both customers and our beloved operators.


Stitching

 For every order, we approach with a high attention and care in sewing and stitching processess. We have a wide variety of sewing machines from China, Bangladesh under IMB brands and aligned through INA intelligent systems to run the production. After each operation, garment is regularly checked by quality controllers for perfect completion.


Each bartack is nicely done to give enhancement and form the basic element to a pair of jeans.


Our workers are well trained to shoot each needle with threads to make each garment in accordance with our quality standard which ensures each garment comes out in consistence, both the spec measurement and sewing craftsmanship. Most important, they are happy working here!


Cutting

 Before marking and cutting, the fabrics must be spread on the cutting table. All rolls must be from the same fabric dye lot. The plies of fabric are placed exactly on top of each other. Care is taken to mark defects and piece ends.


Rolls of fabrics are laid on these boards for cutting precisely following the paper pattern.


Our cutting team consists of experienced cutters and can work with different fabrics like solid, knit, jersey, denim, twill, check, striped and so on. Once the fabric is laid for cutting, the marker is positioned to provide the cutter with an outline of each pattern piece. The markers are arranged to allow for maximum usage of the fabric, and our quality controllers control the cut fabric to be with as little waste as possible.


Washing & Drying

 F17 Jeans is a jeans manufacturer focusing on denim washing. One of our goal is to make every pieces of jeans comes out with natural washing effect just like a person who worn it out in daily life.


We see us as part of the society but not just a jeans manufactory, so we take the responsibility to protect our natural environment. Each of the washing process are using eco-friendly material and we have lots of procedure to deal with the wasted water under HUBER system and let them recycled to protect the environment from pollution.


A deep study onto FABRIC will be conducted before developing the new wash to analyze possibility and potentials of outcomes may achieve with different wash treatments. This covers fabric construction, color fades, shrinkage etc…


Finishing

 The distressed jeans that we see around everywhere undergo a wide variety of wet as well as dry treatments to get the desired effects.Destroying denims is as much an art as it is a technique. Therefore, in our finishing department, we do whiskering, sand blasting, P.P spraying, sponging / brushing and other destroying methods using different types of mashines used for destroy the garments.


Final quality controllers check the measurements on each individual garment to make sure they come out in the right fit and measurements. Next, we attach accessories such buttons / rivets / labels / tags / size cards and etc...


After trimming is finished, using steam set lines and ironing tables, we actively do ironing garments and shape them a new life to bring a good product appearance and pack.
