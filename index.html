February 17 Jeans Textile Company

The company "F17 Jeans" was founded Tashkent, Uzbekistan in 2021. Mission of the company is to produce both denim and non-denim apparels for high-brand customers.

Today, our company is the official brand in the textile world with automation of the production process for sustainable, rich-quality and fashionable garments.
